Meet Our Team

The passionate people behind your perfect cup

Sarah Johnson
Founder & Head Roaster

A former graphic designer who discovered her true calling in coffee. Sarah trained with master roasters in Seattle before opening The Artists' Roast. Her eye for detail, honed through years of design work, now ensures every batch of beans meets our exacting standards.

Marcus Chen
Head Barista

With championships in latte art competitions across the East Coast, Marcus brings both technical precision and artistic flair to every cup. His innovative approach to espresso extraction has earned recognition from Coffee Review Magazine.

Emma Rodriguez
Pastry Chef

Trained at the Culinary Institute of America, Emma creates our daily selection of pastries and light meals. Her seasonal menu reflects her commitment to local ingredients and her Latin American heritage, bringing unique flavors to traditional café fare.

Alex Thompson
Community Manager

A local musician and event organizer, Alex curates our weekly programming and manages artist exhibitions. Their deep connections in Brooklyn's creative community have made The Artists' Roast a genuine cultural hub for emerging talent.
